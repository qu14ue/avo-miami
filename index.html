Our Commitment

What sets us apart.

These aren't just marketing claims. They are commitments you can verify with every visit.

01

Zero Seed Oils — Olive Oil Only

Zero canola oil. Zero sunflower oil. Zero soybean oil. Only Extra Virgin Olive Oil—in everything, always. Because seed oils are the hidden ingredient most "healthy" restaurants keep from you.

02

Dietitian-Designed Meals

Our menu wasn’t designed by a marketing agency or an algorithm. It was crafted by a Registered Dietitian. Every dish is built to nourish you, not just to look good on Instagram.

03

No Microwaves

Nothing at Avo is ever reheated in a microwave. Everything you eat was cooked today. You can see it for yourself. That’s the difference between "marketing fresh" and truly fresh.

04

Grass-Fed + Wild-Caught

The proteins we use matter as much as how we cook them. Grass-fed beef. Wild-caught fish. No factory farms, no cages, no compromises.

05

Made Fresh Daily

No freezers. No preservatives. No Sunday prep served on a Thursday. Every morning we start from scratch—at all three locations.

06

Open Kitchen

We have nothing to hide because we have nothing to conceal. Our kitchen is open. Come, look, and ask. Transparency isn’t a slogan—it’s our architecture.
